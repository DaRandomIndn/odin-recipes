Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for a classic italian lasagna, it includes a savory meat sauce with a classic cheese filling and perfectly cooked pasta

Ingredients

  • Lasagna (Dry Pasta)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground Beef Or Turkey for a leaner meat choice
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded Mixed Cheddar cheeses
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian seasoning

Directions

  1. Boil the dry pasta following the box instructions
  2. Saute onions in a pan
  3. Brown the meat of choice in the same pan as the onions
  4. Season the mixture with garlic, salt and pepper to taste
  5. Add in the tomato sauce can
  6. Fill the tomato sauce can half way with water and put that in as well
  7. Add in Italian seasoning
  8. bring the sauce to a simmer
  9. Mix the ricotta cheese with pepper and italian seasoning
  10. Remove the pasta from the water and place in an oven safe high walled container
  11. Firstly layer in sauce on the bottom of the container
  12. Begin layering the pasta cheese and sauce mixture in that order
  13. End with a layer of sauce on top and top off with mixed cheddar cheese
