Pizza Recipe

Description

A nice and simple at home cheese pizza perfect for a lazy night in

Ingredients

  • Ready Made Pizza Dough
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian seasoning

Directions

  1. Pre heat your oven according to the ready made dough packaging
  2. Warm your pizza sauce slowly
  3. Add salt pepper and Italian seasoning to taste
  4. When the oven is warm and sauce is slightly above room temp
  5. Begin ladling the sauce on the crust in an even manner
  6. Tear and add the Mozzarella cheese evenly
  7. Tear and add basil sprigs atop
  8. Bake until cheese begins to melt making sure to not burn the Basil
  9. Carefully take out the pizza and cut into even slices and Enjoy!
